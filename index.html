How It Works

From setup to render in three steps

Takes is designed to disappear — set it up once, then let the cascade do the thinking for you.

Organize

Build your Takes Tree — group scenes and view layers into a meaningful hierarchy. Add versions for iterations. Tag for filtering and rule automation.

Override

Assign cameras, worlds, actions, render presets, and variant states at any cascade tier. Globals flow down. Local overrides stay local. Zero duplication.

Render

Hit Render. The batch queue walks the tree, applies every override, renders each View Layer into its Smart Output path. You get a coffee. It just works.
